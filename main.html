Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction thriller that explores the concept of dreams within dreams. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, is given a final chance at redemption by performing the impossible—**inception**, planting an idea into someone's subconscious. The movie takes viewers through complex dream layers, action-packed sequences, and mind-bending twists.
